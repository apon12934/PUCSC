Hackathon Challenge Overview

Hackathon Announcement: The Redaction Challenge

Welcome to our upcoming hackathon! This event will focus on building intelligent systems that can automatically redact sensitive information from text data. Participants will work with a specially prepared dataset and will design models or algorithms that identify and conceal confidential elements while preserving the overall readability and utility of the content.

Theme Overview:

Data privacy and responsible information sharing are more important than ever. In this challenge, you'll tackle real-world scenarios where sensitive details must be protected ‚Äî think names, IDs, locations, or other private data ‚Äî without losing the essence of the document.

Example of Redaction:

Original:
John Smith's social security number is 123-45-6789 and he lives in New York.
Redacted:
[NAME]'s social security number is [SSN] and he lives in [LOCATION].

This demonstrates how a redaction system hides sensitive information while keeping the text meaningful.

What to Expect:

  • A dataset will be provided at the start of the contest.
  • Your goal will be to build and evaluate automated redaction techniques.
  • Evaluation criteria will focus on accuracy, coverage, and preservation of context.

To-Do List:

  • Prepare a presentation ‚Äì Summarize your approach, methodology, and results clearly.
  • Maintain a repository for the whole codebase ‚Äì Ensure your project is well-organized, documented, and version-controlled.

Details to Come:

We'll share full dataset access, submission format, and scoring guidelines once the contest begins. Until then, start brainstorming creative ways to balance privacy protection and information integrity!

Frequently Asked Questions

The hackathon will be held on November 28-29, 2025, starting at 08:00 PM at the Hall Room, Presidency University.
Teams must consist of exactly 3 members. You can form teams with your friends or classmates before registration. All team members' information must be provided during registration.

All participants will receive an exclusive event T-shirt, premium notebook, branded pen, and an official participation certificate. Winning teams will receive additional prizes.

Bring your laptop with necessary development tools installed, chargers, student ID, and your creative mindset! Internet will be provided at the venue.
